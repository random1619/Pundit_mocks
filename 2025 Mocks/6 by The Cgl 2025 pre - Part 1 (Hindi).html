x - 3y = -4 {Equation 2}
Solving Equation 1 and Equation 2:
x=y+2 = 3y-4
y = 3
Substituting y = 3 in Equation 1:

Solution

Explanation: The given sentence is in active voice, where the subject "They" is general and does not specify a particular person or group. The sentence follows this structure:
Active Voice Structure:

  • They [Sub] + say [V] + that the earth is round [Obj].

Passive Voice Structure:

  • It [Introductory Subject] + is said [Passive Verb] + that the earth is round [Retained Clause].

Thus, the correct transformation is: "It is said that the earth is round."
