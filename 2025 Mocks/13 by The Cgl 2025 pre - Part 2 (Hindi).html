What was the day of the week on 23rd February 1995?
Thursday
Friday
Saturday
Wednesday
Solution
Remember the code –>
1 4 4 0 2 5 0 3 6 1 4 6 → for Non-Leap Year
J F M A M J J A S O N D → All months of the year
Now,
Add the date, month code, last 2 digits of the year and the no. of leap years.
Date → 23
Month code → 4
Last 2 digits of the year {1995}→ 95
No. of leap years → 23 {Just divide the Last 2 digits of the year by 4 and write the quotient}
Now, 23 + 4 + 95 + 23 = 145
Now just find the no. of odd days = 145/7 = 5 {Remainder}
If No. of odd days comes out to be
01 → Sunday
02 → Monday
03 → Tuesday
04 → Wednesday
05 → Thursday
06 → Friday
00 → Saturday
Here in this case, the odd no. comes out to be 5
So, the day of the week on 23rd February 1995 was Thursday.
Note:- The trick changed a little bit for leap years and other centuries i.e., 1800s, 2000s, etc.
