The RBI governor controls the monetary policy decisions with the support and advice of the internal team and the technical advisory committee.
    MPC has six members - the RBI Governor (Chairperson), the RBI Deputy Governor in charge of monetary policy, one official nominatedby the RBI Board, and the other three would represent the Government of India.

    The decision of the MPC would be binding on the RBI.
