Voluntary Unemployment: Voluntary Unemployment refer to the situation where individuals actively choose not to work, even jobs are available that they are qualified to perform.

The only justifications ___(5)___ by the judge are that the SMA itself provides that when the parties are within the degrees of prohibited relationship, it shall not be valid.

Choose the correct option to fill in the blank 5 -

    offered

    denied

    provide    

    conclude

Solution

The sentence needs a verb denoting past tense hence option C & D will be eliminated. Now as per general sense, Justifications are offered by people. Denied won’t provide the correct sense here. Hence, A is the correct answer.
